Kyle's Portfolio

Below is a collection of projects I've been directly involved. They are comprised of instructional work for companies or customers I've worked for.

You'll find most of my work is not goofy or cartoonish. It's professional and aimed at a wide audience. Certainly, I believe in having fun. But I am certain a level of professionalism build trust. It's a reflection of my experience working with adults in the market research, healthcare, automobile, financial, and manufacturing industries. With the wide audience, I've had to learn to be flexible, honest, and humble. My work is a reflection of that.

I can design or select pre-assessments, formative, and summative assessments in a variety of formats that match learning objectives and engages the learner in demonstrating knowledge and skills.
